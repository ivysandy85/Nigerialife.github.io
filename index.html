Nigeria, is a country in West Africa. It is situated between the Sahel to the north and the Gulf of Guinea to the south in the Atlantic Ocean. Nigeria is a multinational country inhabited by more than 250 ethnic groups speaking 500 distinct languages, all identifying with a wide variety of cultures.
